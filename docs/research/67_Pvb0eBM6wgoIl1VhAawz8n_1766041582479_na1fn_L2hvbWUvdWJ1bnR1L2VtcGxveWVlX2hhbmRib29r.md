**About This Handbook**

This handbook is intended to serve as a guide for all employees of Greenists Event Management Platform. It outlines our policies, procedures, and expectations, and provides information on the benefits and resources available to you. The policies described in this handbook are subject to change at the discretion of the company. Any such changes will be communicated to all employees in a timely manner.

**Employment At-Will**

Employment at Greenists is on an "at-will" basis. This means that both you and the company have the right to terminate the employment relationship at any time, with or without cause, and with or without notice, subject to the provisions of the Yemeni Labour Code.

## General Employment Information

Greenists is an equal opportunity employer. We are committed to providing a work environment that is free from discrimination and harassment. **Anti-Harassment and Non-Discrimination**

Greenists is committed to providing a work environment free of all forms of harassment, including sexual harassment. Harassment of any kind will not be tolerated. Any employee who is found to have engaged in harassment will be subject to disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment. [1]

**Accommodations**

**Open Door Policy**

Accurate recording of time worked is the responsibility of every employee. All non-exempt employees are required to accurately record their start and end times for each workday, as well as any meal breaks. Tampering with, altering, or falsifying time records is a serious violation of company policy and will result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

**Pay Periods**

**Overtime**

Overtime work may be required from time to time to meet business needs. All overtime must be approved in advance by your supervisor. Non-exempt employees will be compensated for overtime work in accordance with the Yemeni Labour Code. The overtime rate is 1.5 times the basic hourly wage for work performed on normal working days, and 2 times the basic hourly wage for work performed at night, on the weekly day of rest, or on official holidays. [1]

**Meal and Rest Breaks**

Employees are entitled to a rest period of at least one hour for prayers and meals, ensuring that no continuous work period exceeds five hours. [1]

## Benefits and Leaves of Absence

**Employee Benefits**

Greenists offers a comprehensive benefits package to eligible employees. This includes [List of benefits, e.g., health insurance, social security, etc.]. Further details on each benefit are available from the Human Resources department.

**Vacation**

Employees are entitled to a minimum of 30 days of paid annual leave per year, after completing one year of service. Leave is accrued at a rate of 2.5 days per month. [1]

**Holidays**

All employees are entitled to paid leave on all official public holidays in Yemen. [1]

**Sick Leave**

Employees are entitled to sick leave in accordance with the Yemeni Labour Code. The duration and compensation for sick leave are as follows:

*   First and second months: 100% of full pay
*   Third and fourth months: 85% of full pay
*   Fifth and sixth months: 75% of full pay
*   Seventh and eighth months: 50% of full pay [1]

A medical certificate from a certified medical authority is required to avail sick leave.

**Maternity Leave**

Female employees are entitled to 60 days of maternity leave with full pay. [1]

**Pilgrimage Leave (Hadj)**

After four years of continuous service, Muslim employees are entitled to 20 days of paid leave to perform the Hadj pilgrimage. This leave is granted once during the employee's service with the company. [1]

**Bereavement Leave**

A female employee whose husband passes away is entitled to 40 days of leave with pay. [1]

## Standards of Conduct

**Workplace Violence Prevention**

Greenists is committed to providing a safe and secure workplace. We have a zero-tolerance policy for workplace violence. Any act or threat of violence will be taken seriously and will result in immediate disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment and referral to law enforcement authorities.

**Drug and Alcohol Use**

It is the policy of Greenists to maintain a drug-free and alcohol-free workplace. The use, possession, distribution, or sale of illegal drugs or alcohol on company premises or during working hours is strictly prohibited and will result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

**Smoking**

Smoking is prohibited in all indoor areas of Greenists' facilities. Smoking is only permitted in designated outdoor smoking areas.

**Attendance and Punctuality**

Employees are expected to be punctual and to report to work as scheduled. If you are unable to report to work, you must notify your supervisor as soon as possible. Unexcused absences or tardiness will result in disciplinary action.

**Dress and Grooming Standards**

Employees are expected to dress in a professional and appropriate manner for their roles. For event staff, a specific uniform may be required. Please refer to the specific dress code guidelines for your department.

**Non-Disclosure and Confidentiality**

During the course of your employment, you may have access to confidential information about Greenists, its clients, and its business operations. You are required to maintain the confidentiality of this information, both during and after your employment with the company. Unauthorized disclosure of confidential information will result in disciplinary action, up to and including termination of employment.

**Use of Company Property**

Company property, including but not limited to computers, phones, and vehicles, is to be used for business purposes only. Personal use of company property is not permitted without prior authorization. Employees are expected to take reasonable care of all company property.

**Internet, E-mail, and Electronic Communications**

Company-provided internet, e-mail, and other electronic communication systems are to be used for business purposes. Greenists reserves the right to monitor the use of these systems to ensure compliance with company policies.

**Ethics and Conduct**

All employees are expected to conduct themselves in an ethical and professional manner at all times. This includes treating colleagues, clients, and vendors with respect and honesty.

**Safety**

Greenists is committed to providing a safe and healthy work environment. All employees are expected to follow all safety rules and procedures. Any accidents or unsafe conditions must be reported to your supervisor immediately.

**Visitors in the Workplace**

For security reasons, all visitors must be registered at the reception desk. Employees are responsible for their visitors and must escort them at all times.

## Event-Specific Policies

**On-Site Conduct**

During events, all staff are representatives of Greenists and are expected to maintain the highest standards of professionalism. This includes but is not limited to:

*   Providing excellent customer service to all attendees, clients, and vendors.
*   Adhering to the event schedule and being present at your assigned post on time.
*   Communicating effectively with team members and supervisors.
*   Proactively addressing any issues or concerns that may arise and reporting them to your supervisor.

**Health and Safety at Events**

Given the dynamic nature of events, health and safety are of paramount importance. All employees must adhere to the following:

*   Familiarize yourself with the event's emergency action plan, including evacuation routes and emergency contact information.
*   Report any potential safety hazards to your supervisor immediately.
*   Follow all safety guidelines for setting up, managing, and breaking down event equipment and structures.
*   In the event of a medical emergency, contact the designated on-site medical team and your supervisor immediately.

**Client and Vendor Interaction**

Professional and respectful interaction with clients and vendors is essential. All employees are expected to:

*   Maintain a positive and collaborative relationship with all clients and vendors.
*   Address any client or vendor concerns promptly and professionally.
*   Refer any contractual or financial issues to your supervisor or the appropriate department.

**Use of Event Equipment**

All event equipment is the property of Greenists or its clients and must be handled with care. Employees are responsible for:

*   Using all equipment in accordance with its intended purpose and safety guidelines.
*   Reporting any damaged or malfunctioning equipment to your supervisor immediately.
*   Ensuring all equipment is securely stored and accounted for at the end of each event.
