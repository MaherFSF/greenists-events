## Standard Operating Procedure: Corporate Event Planning

This document outlines the standard operating procedure for planning and executing corporate events in Aden, Yemen, for the Greenists Event Management Platform.

### 1. Initial Client Consultation

*   **Objective:** To understand the client's vision, goals, and requirements for the event.
*   **Steps:**
    1.  Schedule and conduct an initial meeting with the client.
    2.  Discuss the event's purpose, target audience, and desired outcomes.
    3.  Gather information on the event's budget, timeline, and preferred date.
    4.  Inquire about any specific themes, branding requirements, or cultural considerations.

### 2. Venue Selection and Booking

*   **Objective:** To identify and secure a suitable venue for the event.
*   **Steps:**
    1.  Based on the client's requirements, research and shortlist potential venues in Aden.
    2.  Contact the shortlisted venues to check for availability and pricing.
    3.  Arrange for site visits for the client to inspect the venues.
    4.  Negotiate the terms of the contract and book the selected venue.

### 3. Supplier and Vendor Management

*   **Objective:** To engage and coordinate with various suppliers and vendors for the event.
*   **Steps:**
    1.  Identify the required suppliers and vendors (e.g., catering, audiovisual, decoration, entertainment).
    2.  Source and vet potential suppliers based on their reputation and experience.
    3.  Request quotations and negotiate prices.
    4.  Finalize the contracts and coordinate the logistics with the selected suppliers.

### 4. Event Marketing and Promotion

*   **Objective:** To create awareness and generate interest in the event.
*   **Steps:**
    1.  Develop a marketing and promotion plan.
    2.  Design and produce marketing materials (e.g., invitations, brochures, social media content).
    3.  Distribute the marketing materials through various channels.
    4.  Manage the registration process and track the number of attendees.

### 5. On-site Event Management

*   **Objective:** To ensure the smooth execution of the event on the day.
*   **Steps:**
    1.  Develop a detailed event timeline and run-of-show.
    2.  Coordinate with the venue staff, suppliers, and vendors.
    3.  Oversee the setup and decoration of the venue.
    4.  Manage the registration and check-in process.
    5.  Handle any issues or emergencies that may arise during the event.

### 6. Post-event Evaluation

*   **Objective:** To assess the success of the event and gather feedback for future improvements.
*   **Steps:**
    1.  Conduct a post-event debrief with the client and the event team.
    2.  Collect feedback from the attendees through surveys or interviews.
    3.  Analyze the event's performance against the set goals and objectives.
    4.  Prepare a post-event report with key findings and recommendations.
