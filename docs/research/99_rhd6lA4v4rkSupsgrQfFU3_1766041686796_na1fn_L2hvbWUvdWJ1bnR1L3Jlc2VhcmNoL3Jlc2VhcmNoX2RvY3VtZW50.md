## 2. Provider Details

### 2.1. Sohobcom (سُحبكم)

Sohobcom is a national cloud service provider in Yemen. They offer 'Email as a Service' with various plans. While their main office is in Sana'a, their services are cloud-based and should be accessible from Aden. They have a WhatsApp number for contact, which is a common communication channel in Yemen.

### 2.2. TeleYemen (تيليمن)

TeleYemen is the national telecommunications company in Yemen. They offer a 'POP Domain' service for company emails. They have a confirmed branch in Aden, making them a strong candidate for local support. Their pricing is provided in Yemeni Rial.

### 2.3. Sakhr Net Technology (صخر نت تكنولوجي)

Sakhr Net Technology is a web hosting company that includes email accounts in their hosting plans. Their pricing is in USD and billed annually. Their office is in Sana'a, and it is not clear if they have a presence or direct support in Aden.
