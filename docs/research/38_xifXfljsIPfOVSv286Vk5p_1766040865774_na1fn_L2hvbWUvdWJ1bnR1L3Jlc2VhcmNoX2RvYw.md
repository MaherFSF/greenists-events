## Services

Al-Qutaibi Islamic Bank offers a range of services, including:

*   **Mobile Banking:** The bank provides a mobile banking application called "Al-Qutaibi Mobile" [2].
*   **Cards:** The bank issues Mastercard debit cards. The issuance fee is $18, and international withdrawals are available with a 4% fee. The daily cash withdrawal limits are 200,000 YER, 1,000 SAR, and 300 USD [3].
*   **SWIFT:** The bank's SWIFT code is **ALMFYESAXXX** [4]. This service is currently available for corporate clients only.
*   **Money Transfers:** The bank offers local and international money transfer services.
*   **Financing:** The bank provides various financing products, including investment, real estate, agricultural, and small business financing.

## Sharia Compliance

Al-Qutaibi Islamic Bank operates as an Islamic bank, which means its products and services are compliant with Sharia law. The bank has a Sharia supervisory board to ensure compliance.
