## 3. Services

Al-Kuraimi Bank offers a wide range of financial services, including:

*   **Money Transfer:** The bank provides both domestic and international money transfer services. Domestic transfers are facilitated through "Kuraimi Express," while international transfers are available through various partners, including Western Union and a SWIFT network.
*   **Accounts:** The bank offers various types of accounts, including current accounts, savings accounts, and investment deposit accounts.
*   **Financing:** Al-Kuraimi Bank provides financing options for individuals and businesses, adhering to Islamic finance principles.
*   **Digital Banking:** The bank has several mobile applications, including "Kuraimi Jawal" and "MFloos," which allow customers to manage their accounts and perform transactions remotely.
