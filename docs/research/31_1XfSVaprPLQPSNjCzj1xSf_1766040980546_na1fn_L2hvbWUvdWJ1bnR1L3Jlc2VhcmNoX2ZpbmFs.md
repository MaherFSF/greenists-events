# Private Security Companies for Events in Aden, Yemen

This document provides an overview of private security companies operating in Aden, Yemen, with a focus on their suitability for event security. The information has been gathered from publicly available sources and is intended to be a starting point for further engagement with these companies.

## Key Findings

The private security landscape in Yemen is complex, with many companies having a primary presence in the capital, Sana'a, while maintaining operations in other key cities like Aden. Direct contact information for Aden-specific branches can be challenging to obtain through online research alone. The companies listed below have been identified as having a presence or offering services in Aden.

## Pricing

Pricing information for security services in Yemen is not publicly available and is typically provided upon request and after a detailed security assessment. It is recommended to contact the companies directly to obtain a quote tailored to specific event requirements. Prices will vary based on the number of guards, the duration of the event, the level of risk, and the specific services required.
