## 2. International Providers with Presence in the Region

### 2.1. FileCloud

FileCloud is an enterprise cloud storage solution that offers data residency in the Middle East (Bahrain, UAE), which could be a viable option for Greenists.

*   **Website:** [https://www.filecloud.com/](https://www.filecloud.com/)

FileCloud offers two main products:

*   **FileCloud Server:** Self-hosted on-premise.
*   **FileCloud Online:** SaaS solution hosted by FileCloud.

Pricing for FileCloud Online starts at $10/user/month for the Standard plan (minimum 5 users).
