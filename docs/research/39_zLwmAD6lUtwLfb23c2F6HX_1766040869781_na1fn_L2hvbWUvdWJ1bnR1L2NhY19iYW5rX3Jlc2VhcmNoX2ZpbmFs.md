## 2. Corporate Accounts

CAC Bank offers corporate current accounts designed to help businesses manage their finances effectively. These accounts provide access to a wide range of banking products and services.

### 2.1. Features

*   **Nationwide Access:** Use the account at any CAC Bank branch or office throughout Yemen.
*   **ATM Card:** Withdraw cash from any ATM or pay at any POS terminal within the bank's network.
*   **E-Banking:** Access a suite of electronic services, including:
    *   Letters of Credit (LCs)
    *   Foreign Transfers
    *   International Guarantees
*   **24/7 Access:** Manage banking operations at any time.
*   **Security:** High levels of confidentiality and data encryption.
*   **Online Statements:** View and print account statements online.

### 2.2. Requirements

**For Sole Proprietorships:**

*   Valid Commercial Registration.
*   Valid electronic national ID card of the owner.
*   For foreign residents: a copy of the passport, residence card, and a letter from the employer.

**For Corporations:**

*   Attested incorporation contract.
*   Establishment resolution.
*   Attested memorandum and articles of association.
*   A board resolution authorizing the account opening and specifying the authorized signatories.
*   Valid electronic national ID cards of the owners and authorized signatories.
*   Completed account opening application forms.
*   **Minimum Opening Balance:** YR 300,000 (or its equivalent in other currencies).

## 3. International Transfers

CAC Bank's International Operations division, based in Aden, facilitates international trade and payment services. The bank has established strong relationships with financial institutions globally to ensure smooth transactions for its clients.

### 3.1. Services

*   **Trade Finance:**
    *   Import and Export Letters of Credit (LCs)
    *   Standby Letters of Credit (SBLCs)
    *   Documentary Collections
*   **Payments:**
    *   Outward/Inward SWIFT Transfers (for trade purposes only)
*   **Other Services:**
    *   Foreign Exchange Services
    *   Treasury & Investments
    *   Issuance of Guarantees

### 3.2. Process

The process for international transfers is initiated through the bank's branches and managed by the International Operations division in Aden. The bank utilizes the SWIFT network for these transactions. For detailed procedures and pricing, it is recommended to contact the International Operations department directly at **00967 2 297131**.
